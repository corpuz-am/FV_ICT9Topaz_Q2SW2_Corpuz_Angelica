Itinerary

Visual Representation Days and Activities
Day 1:
  1. Have Coffee at Phil's Brew; Located at Amboy Cor Argonza Sts, Basco, Luzon Philippines
  2. Visit and Have a Relaxing Stroll at the Beautiful Valugan Boulder Beach
  3. Have Lunch at the Italian Restaurant Called Casa Napoli; Located at Brgy. Chanarian, Basco, Luzon 3900 Philippines
  4. Visit the Tukon Chapel which is known for it's Peaceful Ambiance and Beautiful Stones
  5. Have Dinner at Vunong Dinette which is known for their Native Cuisine giving you a Authentic Experience
Day 2:
  1. Have Breakfast at Beehan's Meals & Snack known for their Extensive choices of Ivatan food
  2. Visit the Basco Lighthouse at Naidi Hills which is a perfect spot for Sunset Viewing
  3. Have Lunch at Cafe du Tukon; Located at Tulan Sitio Tukon Chanarian, Tukon, Basco, Luzon Philippines
  4. Visit the Dipnaysuhuan Japanese Tunnel which is known for to be filled with history as it served as a shelter for Japanese soldiers when they arrived in Batanes in 1941
  5. Have Dinner at Yaru Art Gallery & Fip Cafe which is known for their Great Food accompanied with an even better view
Day 3:
  1. Have Breakfast at Hiro's Cafe; Located at Abad Cor. La Fuente Sts, Basco, Luzon 3900 Philippines
  2. Visit Basco Rolling Hills which is known for its Picturesque hills with views & animals
  3. Have Lunch at Jino’s East Pizzaria that features delicious and great homemade pizzas
  4. Relax and Unwind while you visit the Chawa View Deck known for its amazing view of the mountains and sea
  5. Have Dinner at Pension Ivatan that offers delectable and mouth-watering Ivatan cuisines and other Filipino favorites.
